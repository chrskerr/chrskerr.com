Cat Game

  • Play (iframe in modal)
  • GitHub repo

The Cat Game was a week two piece of homework that I got a little too carried away with. The initial assignment was to make the cat graphic bounce from left-to-right from the edges of the screen, and a suggestion that we can try to make it more exciting.

Thus the game was born.

Instructions:

  • Move the Mouse with the arrow keys
  • Your goal is to eat as many pieces of cheese as possible, without being eaten by a cat
  • You can hide in the walls and wrap around to the opposite side of the board, the cats cannot

Largely untouched since finishing, for nostaglic reasons.

This game is not currently mobile responsive, as it wasn't when it was written.
